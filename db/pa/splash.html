Introducing FindMyRide PA
rabbittransit, in partnership with PennDOT, is developing the FindMyRidePA trip planner to improve transportation services for veterans and their families by making it easier to identify their transportation options and plan their trips. The new design will let you use your computer or smart phone to quickly and easily identify your best choices for making a trip to places like the doctorâ€™s office or a job interview. You can decide if your best choice is public transit, paratransit, volunteer vans or a taxi using FindMyRidePA, your one-stop transportation information resource.
When will it be Available?
The FindMyRidePA software will be pilot tested in York County in the summer of 2014, and expanded to a six-county region (York, Adams, Cumberland, Dauphin, Lebanon and Franklin) in the fall of 2014. Thirty kiosks will be deployed at locations identified specifically for veterans, service members, their families and others with transportation needs. Eventually, we expect the program to expand statewide.
Real Information
Transportation authorities, service providers and brokers will use the FindMyRidePA software to maintain and update information on their services, including geographic coverage, vehicle types, disabilities that can be accommodated, fares, and program eligibility.The program will also link to existing transportation information services including: PA 5-1-1; PA 2-1-1; PACommutes; and PA Commuter Services.
